Fit Tips

Exercise Daily: Even just a short walk!
You should have some sort of moderate physical activity in your everyday life. If you're looking to shed pounds fast, do a higher-level intensity workout and set intervals to sprint. Your muscles may ache after; that means your body is changing, for the better. Stay hydrated, stretch, and eat foods with protein after each workout.
Eat Right: Abs are made in the kitchen
Try to stay away from sweets. Sugar will not help you get in shape, even a single candy bar. Fruits and vegetables are best when getting into shape. Apples, for example, do a good job at making the stomach feel full for up to 3 to 4 hours. Green vegetables, green beans and broccoli, keep the digestive system clean and running.
Track Intake: Calories AND water
Keeping track of how many calories you eat in a day helps planning your physical exercising. Ever wonder why body builders' body masses are so big? That's because they plan out their meals and take in more (healthy) calories than the average person.
Sleep Well: Don't need to say this twice
It is crucial to get enough sleep to recharge the body's batteries. 6-8 hours of sleep will keep the body going throughout the day, but if you happen to feel tired at any point after coming home from work, take a small nap before exercising.
Stay Motivated: Tomorrow, you'll thank yourself
An important key to being in shape is to set goals and keep a positive mindset. If you stay positive, you will be able to push yourself to get that fit body you've always wanted.
